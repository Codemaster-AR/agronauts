By 2050, our planet will have nearly 10 billion people to feed, but our available farmland is shrinking. We need a smarter way to grow.

Every year, over $5.4$ quadrillion liters of water are used in farming fruits and vegetables, an unfathomable amount. For comparison, that volume is more than enough to fill the entire Grand Canyon. This critical problem, along with its profound consequences, is often overlooked.
